About Jet Lag

Jet lag occurs when your body's internal clock (circadian rhythm) is out of sync with the local time at your destination. It's caused by rapid travel across multiple time zones. Symptoms include fatigue, insomnia, difficulty concentrating, and digestive issues. Flying east is generally harder than flying west because it's easier for your body to extend the day than to shorten it.

Tips to Avoid Baggage Fees
  • Pack light and use carry-on only when possible (saves $30–$100+ per trip)
  • Consider airline credit cards — many offer 1–2 free checked bags
  • Elite frequent flyer status often includes free checked bags
  • Weigh your bag at home — overweight fees ($75–$200) dwarf standard fees
  • Southwest Airlines still offers 2 free checked bags for all passengers
